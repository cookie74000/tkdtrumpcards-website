FAQ

Common questions

Do I need any technical skills to set this up? ▼
None at all. We build and set up everything for you. You just send us your student list and we handle the rest. Your admin panel is simple enough that anyone can use it — updating a belt grade takes about 10 seconds.
How do I get paid? ▼
We set up a Stripe account connected to your bank. When a student pays £2.99, it goes directly into your Stripe account. We never touch your money — we don't take a percentage of student payments. Standard Stripe transaction fees (typically 1.4% + 20p per transaction) apply.
What happens when a student gets a new belt? ▼
You log into your admin panel and update their belt grade. Their card stats update automatically — no extra charge, no waiting. The student's card immediately reflects their new grade.
What if a student leaves the club? ▼
You can archive their card from the admin panel. It disappears from the active game but is kept on record. When a new student joins, you add them and they pay their £2.99. The game keeps earning as your club grows and changes.
Does this work for martial arts other than Taekwondo? ▼
Absolutely. The game works for any martial art with a belt grading system — Karate, Kickboxing, BJJ, Judo, and more. We customise the card design and stats to match your discipline. Just mention your martial art when you enquire.
Is there a monthly fee? ▼
No. The £99.99 is a one-time licence fee. There are no monthly fees, no annual renewals, no hidden costs. You pay once and the game is yours to run indefinitely.
How long does it take to go live? ▼
Typically 48–72 hours after we receive your student list and branding details. We'll send you a preview link to approve before it goes live to your students.

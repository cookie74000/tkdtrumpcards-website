The Process

From purchase to live game in 5 steps

01

Fill in the form below

Tell us your club name, martial art, and how many students you have. We'll reply within 24 hours with a payment link.

One-time payment via Stripe. No monthly fees, no hidden costs. Your licence covers unlimited students.

03

A simple spreadsheet with student names, belt grades, and training years. We'll send you a template to fill in.

We set up your branded game with all your students as cards. Takes 48–72 hours. You'll get a preview link to approve before launch.

What We Need From You

Getting your game ready

📋 Student List Format

We'll send you a spreadsheet template. You'll need to fill in:

Student first name and last initial (e.g. Jamie K.)
Current belt grade (e.g. Black Belt 1st Dan)
Approximate years of training
Competition wins (optional — boosts stats)
Student photo (optional at launch — silhouette used until uploaded)

🎨 Club Branding

To customise your game's look:

Club name (as it should appear on cards)
Club logo (PNG or SVG preferred)
Primary colour (hex code or describe it)
Any specific styling preferences

Don't worry if you don't have all of this — we can work with whatever you have and make it look great.
